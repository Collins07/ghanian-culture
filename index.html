Akan Child Naming System

According to Akan tradition, each child is born with a name associated to their soul.
The day the child is born is therefore determined by the soul. The well thought and organized
naming system of the Akan’s was initiated by the ancestors and has attracted so many
people’s attention as they desire to know more about these names.

Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa

Thursady Yaw Yaa
Friday Kofi Afua
Saturday Kwame Ame

As a matter of fact, it is possible to tell what led to someone’s birth, the day they were born and their
rank among the siblings just by their names. This is why you need to
